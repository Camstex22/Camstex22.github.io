Why This Matters

Professional driving — whether you're running long-haul freight across three states or hitting 80 delivery stops a day — puts a unique and compounding strain on the human body that general health advice simply doesn't account for. Fast-food diets and gas-station energy drinks wreck the oral microbiome and disrupt hormonal regulation. The combination creates a whole-body health burden that accumulates silently until it sidelines a driver entirely. A trucker wellness program has to address all of these systems together — not just one in isolation.
