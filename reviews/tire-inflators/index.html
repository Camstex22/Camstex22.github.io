The blowout steering response drill walks you through the correct sequence: maintain or briefly accelerate to stabilize the vehicle, steer straight, then gradually reduce speed by easing off the throttle rather than braking, and only apply gentle braking once the vehicle is stable. Practicing this in a mental simulation — which the guide structures as a step-by-step visualization drill — builds the muscle memory response needed to override panic instinct. A full roadside response protocol rounds out the package, covering hazard light positioning, safe vehicle placement, and when to call for assistance rather than change the tire yourself.
