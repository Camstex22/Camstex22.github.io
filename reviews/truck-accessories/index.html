★★★★☆ 4.5 / 5 — Best Budget Pick

CURT is the leading name in aftermarket trailer hitches, and the Class III 13055 is their most versatile and popular receiver hitch for full-size pickup trucks. Featuring a standard 2-inch receiver opening, this hitch is compatible with the vast majority of trailer accessories, bike racks, cargo carriers, and ball mounts on the market today. The solid-steel construction is A-grade welded and finished with a black powder coat that's held up beautifully even after multiple years of exposure to road salt and moisture in our testing.

The 13055 is rated for a gross trailer weight of 6,000 lbs and a tongue weight of 750 lbs — perfectly suited for hauling a boat, camper, utility trailer, or jet skis on weekends. Installation uses existing frame holes on most compatible trucks, meaning there's no drilling required in many cases, though some truck models may need minor adjustment. CURT provides model-specific fitment guides, and we found the hardware to be of excellent quality with no stripped threads or fitment issues during installation.

The one area where CURT could improve is the finish durability at the weld points — after extended exposure to harsh conditions, we noticed minor surface rust beginning around welds, though this was purely cosmetic and did not compromise structural integrity. A quick touch-up with rust-resistant paint resolved it completely. For the price, the CURT 13055 is an exceptional value that opens up an enormous world of towing and hauling capabilities for your pickup truck.

Our Verdict: The CURT 13055 is the hitch we recommend to anyone adding towing capability to their truck. Reliable, affordable, and compatible with hundreds of accessories — it's an essential truck upgrade.
