Rough Country 3-Inch Leveling Lift Kit

Suspension / Lift Kit
★★★★★ 4.7 / 5.0 (5,800+ reviews) $85 – $150

If you want to fit aggressive all-terrain tires and eliminate the factory rake without breaking the bank, Rough Country's 3-Inch Leveling Kit is the single best value in the pickup truck suspension market right now. It ships with CNC-machined billet aluminum spacers and includes all the hardware you need, making it a favorite among weekend DIY mechanics and off-road enthusiasts alike.

We installed this on a 2024 Ford F-150 and a 2023 Ram 1500 during our testing phase. Both installs were completed with basic hand tools in about two hours per truck, and the result was a commanding, level stance that allowed us to run up to 35-inch tires with no rubbing. Ride quality saw a negligible change on pavement, though we did notice slightly increased road feel on washboard dirt roads — acceptable given the price point.

One caveat worth noting: this kit does not include new front shocks, which are recommended if your originals have more than 40,000 miles on them. Rough Country sells compatible shocks separately. Also, always re-align your front end after installation — a step some budget-focused buyers overlook but that makes a real difference in tire wear and handling.

Best for: Truck owners who want to run larger tires and achieve an aggressive stance on a tight budget without sacrificing trail capability.
